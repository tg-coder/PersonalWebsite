Maltese Dogs

Maltese Dogs

Maltese are really cute dogs. They have generally white fluffy fur, tiny paws and lots of energy. They aren't generally the type of dogs who bite since they have a very small mouth, but while they are teething they made bite a little bit just for fun. When people come over, maltese are the dogs who get excited and are friendly towards them. If you want a type of dog who just sits and relaxes in your lap, maltese are just the right breed for you!
